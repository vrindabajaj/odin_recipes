Enchanted Surf and Turf Extravaganza

Description

Embark on a culinary journey into the realms of delight with our Enchanted Surf and Turf Extravaganza—a mystical fusion of land and sea that transcends ordinary dining. This fantastical dish transports you to a world where succulent lobster tails and plump sea scallops dance alongside tender filet mignon steaks, creating a harmony of flavors fit for royalty.

Sea Symphony:

Lobster tails, adorned with a luscious olive oil and garlic elixir, take center stage on a celestial baking sheet. Paired with the ethereal sweetness of large sea scallops, they undergo a transformative bake, emerging from the oven as a sea symphony—a harmonious blend of flavors heightened by a splash of white wine and a zesty squeeze of fresh lemon juice. A sprinkling of chopped parsley serves as the final touch, a verdant tribute to the ocean's bounty.

Land Legends:

Meanwhile, the terrestrial legends, filet mignon steaks, bask in the warmth of olive oil and the aromatic embrace of fresh rosemary. Searing to perfection in a skillet, these succulent steaks embody the essence of the earth's richness, offering a robust and tender contrast to the maritime wonders on the plate.

Magical Sauce Alchemy:

The crowning glory of this culinary odyssey is the Magical Sauce—a concoction of butter, garlic, lemon zest, and parsley that undergoes a mystical transformation in the skillet. As it simmers, the sauce transcends the ordinary, becoming a golden elixir that cascades over the surf and turf, enchanting each morsel with an irresistible symphony of flavors. The sauce, a testament to culinary alchemy, ties the elements together in a magical embrace.

Presentation:

Gathered on a regal platter, the enchanted surf and turf beckons with its vibrant colors and enticing aromas. Drizzled with the bewitching sauce and adorned with fresh parsley, it invites you to partake in a feast fit for the most discerning palates. Lemon wedges stand ready to add a citrusy spark, elevating the dining experience to new heights.

Embark on this gastronomic adventure, where sea and land converge in a mystical union, creating a dining experience that transcends the ordinary and transports you to a realm of culinary enchantment.

Ingredients

For the Surf (Sea):

  • 4 lobster tails, cleaned and halved
  • 1 pound large sea scallops
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup white wine
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish

For the Turf (Land):

  • 4 filet mignon steaks
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 sprigs of fresh rosemary

Magical Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Prepare the Surf (Sea):
    • Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
    • In a bowl, mix olive oil, minced garlic, salt, and pepper. Brush the lobster tails and scallops with this mixture.
    • Place the lobster tails and scallops on a baking sheet. Drizzle white wine and lemon juice over them.
    • Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the lobster is opaque and the scallops are golden brown. Garnish with chopped parsley.
  2. Cook the Turf (Land):
    • Season the filet mignon steaks with salt and pepper.
    • Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add the steaks and sear for 3-4 minutes on each side for medium-rare (adjust cooking time for your preferred doneness).
    • In the last minute of cooking, add fresh rosemary sprigs to infuse flavor. Remove the steaks from the pan and let them rest for a few minutes.
    Prepare the Magical Sauce:
    • In the same skillet used for the steaks, melt butter over medium heat.
    • Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
    • Stir in lemon zest, lemon juice, and chopped parsley. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
    • Let the sauce simmer for a couple of minutes until it thickens slightly.
  3. Assemble:
    • Arrange the lobster tails, scallops, and filet mignon steaks on a serving platter.
    • Drizzle the magical sauce over the top.
    • Garnish with additional fresh parsley and lemon wedges if desired.

Serve this fantasy surf and turf with your favorite side dishes and enjoy a magical culinary experience!
